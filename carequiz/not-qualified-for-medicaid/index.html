You Are Caregiver or Family Memeber

From securing affordable home care to estate planning, MMAG will take care of everything that you and your disabled, aged, and blind loved one needs.

Did you know that the large majority of family members of those of advanced age and disabilities are caregivers?

There are almost 44 million caregivers in the US - but many don’t identify as such. But by not identifying as a caregiver, many caregivers deprive themselves of the resources that areavailable to them.

Although the image conjured with the term ‘caregiver’ is one of a 24-hour aide - the definition is actually a lot more broad than that. A caregiver is someone who takes mom to her doctor’s appointment, helps dad out with his groceries, or stops by an elderly neighbors house to helpthem with some light housekeeping.

Did you know:

  • At least one-third of caregivers struggle financially
  • More than two thirds of caregivers say that caregiving affects their jobs - so much so,that those who care for a parent lose an average of $304,000 in wages and benefits
  • At least 1 in 4 caregivers are in dire need of a break - but don’t know how to access it.
  • The majority of people see caregiving as a ‘labour of love’, leading to feelings of guilt when considering asking for help.
